Shipped · behind switch

Regime filter — noticing the weather

Every strategy is long-only momentum-family; in a decline they all lose or idle together. regime.py: no NEW entries anywhere while SPY < 200-day SMA. Entries only — exits, stops, flattens are never gated. Fails OPEN on missing/shallow data with a loud log (a protective overlay must never become a deadlock class — the 2026-07-07 lesson). State changes are logged, audit-mirrored, phone-notified. Wired twice: main.py holds routed signals; xsection.py skips rotations whole (kill-switch atomicity). REGIME_FILTER=off is the escape hatch.

THE HONEST CAVEAT

Coherent ≠ profitable

Everything fixed this week makes the model measure honestly. None of it makes the model make money. The strategies' thresholds and lookbacks remain exactly as designed — deliberately — and whether that design has an edge is still an open question with zero evidence either way.

Two traps, unchanged: reading the next green or red day as a verdict (single sessions validate mechanics, never edge), and tuning thresholds off daily P&L (the threshold is the strategy; test changes in the backtest, where a bad idea costs seconds).
The pivot point: the tuning phase is essentially complete. From here, the work stops being "fix the machine" and becomes "collect the evidence" — honest paper trades accumulating toward Monte Carlo's 30-trade bar, and the backtest on real history.
